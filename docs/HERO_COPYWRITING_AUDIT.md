# Hero Section Copywriting Audit — Skolar Landing Page

**Date:** 2026-03-05
**Target audience:** Indian parents (mothers primarily) of CBSE Class 1-5 children
**Device split:** 70% mobile

---

## Variation 1: Emotion-Led (Parent's Love)

**Badge:** `CBSE Classes 1–5 · Free to start`

**Headline:** Her practice time shouldn't end in *tears.*

**Subheadline:** Worksheets that match your child's exact CBSE chapter — with easy, medium, and hard questions, Indian names, and a full answer key. Ready in 30 seconds.

**CTA:** Generate a free worksheet

**Chips:** 198 topics · 9 subjects · Classes 1–5 · Verified answers · No app needed

**Why this works:**
- Opens with a scene every Indian mother recognises — homework time turning into a battle
- "Her" makes it personal (mirrors the majority-mother audience)
- Subheadline pivots to rational: exact chapter match, difficulty tiers, Indian context, answer key, speed
- Badge drops "AI-Powered" (scares traditional parents) and leads with the #1 search filter + free
- "Verified answers" directly attacks the pain point of wrong answers on free sites

---

## Variation 2: Problem-Led (Tired of Bad Worksheets) — RECOMMENDED

**Badge:** `CBSE Classes 1–5 · Free to start`

**Headline:** Tired of worksheets with *wrong answers?*

**Subheadline:** Skolar generates CBSE worksheets where every maths answer is verified by code — not copy-pasted from the internet. Three difficulty levels. Indian contexts. Print-ready PDF with answer key.

**CTA:** Generate a free worksheet

**Chips:** 198 topics · 9 subjects · Classes 1–5 · Every answer checked · Hindi worksheets too

**Why this works:**
- Rhetorical question hits the exact pain point Indian parents voice on WhatsApp groups
- "Verified by code" is concrete, believable — more trustworthy than "AI-powered"
- "Not copy-pasted from the internet" is blunt, conversational language real parents use
- "Hindi worksheets too" addresses the second pain point directly
- Subheadline front-loads the differentiator before listing features

---

## Variation 3: Result-Led (Better Marks, More Confidence)

**Badge:** `CBSE Classes 1–5 · Free to start`

**Headline:** From "I can't do this" to *"I got it."*

**Subheadline:** CBSE worksheets that start easy and build confidence — three difficulty levels per topic, hints on hard questions, and every answer verified. Your child practises more because they finally *want* to.

**CTA:** Generate a free worksheet

**Chips:** 198 topics · 9 subjects · Classes 1–5 · 3 difficulty levels · Free to start

**Why this works:**
- Transformation headline captures what every parent actually wants
- "Start easy and build confidence" reframes three-tier difficulty as an emotional benefit
- "Your child practises more because they finally want to" is the ultimate result
- Avoids any mention of "AI" or technical language — pure outcome
- Works best for aspirational parents

---

## Recommendation

**Lead with Variation 2 (Problem-Led)** as default hero:
1. Highest specificity — "wrong answers" is a concrete, searchable pain point
2. Strongest differentiator — "verified by code" is a claim no competitor can make
3. Addresses both key pain points — wrong answers (headline) + Hindi worksheets (chips)

**A/B test Variation 1 (Emotion-Led)** — emotional copy is polarising but converts hard when it hits.

**Save Variation 3 for retargeting ads** — visitors who've already seen the product need a nudge toward the outcome.
